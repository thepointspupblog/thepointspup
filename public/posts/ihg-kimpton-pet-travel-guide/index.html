IHG Pet Policy Guide: Why Kimpton Is the Best Chain for Large Dogs

Complete guide to IHG's pet-friendly hotel brands including Kimpton, Hotel Indigo, Staybridge Suites, and Candlewood Suites. Compare pet fees, weight limits, and learn how to book free stays with points.

Dec 10, 2025
5 minute read

If you’ve been following along, you know I’m obsessed with Hilton for dog travel. But here’s the thing: IHG has a secret weapon that even Hilton can’t match.

It’s called Kimpton.

Kimpton is hands-down the most dog-friendly hotel brand in existence. No weight limits. No breed restrictions. No pet fees. Ever. They’ll welcome your Great Dane the same way they’d welcome a Chihuahua.

But Kimpton is just one piece of the IHG puzzle. Let me break down the entire IHG portfolio so you know exactly where you can (and can’t) bring your dog.

How to Earn IHG Points for Free Stays

Here’s where it gets good. You can stay at Kimpton for free using IHG One Rewards points.

IHG points are worth about 0.5-0.6 cents each. A typical Kimpton redemption runs 30,000-60,000 points per night, though prices vary by date and location.

Pro tip: The 4th night free perk on the Premier card is huge. Book 4 nights and you essentially get 25% off your points redemption.

  3. Use the IHG app filter ‚Äî Search for “Pet Friendly” under amenities, but verify the specific policy.

  4. Stack promotions ‚Äî IHG frequently runs double/triple point promotions. Combine with credit card bonuses for maximum value.

The Bottom Line

If you’re traveling with a large dog and want zero stress about pet policies, Kimpton is unbeatable. No weight limits, no fees, no judgment.

The trade-off? There are only about 70 Kimpton properties, mostly in major cities. You won’t find one in every small town.

For broader coverage, pair your IHG strategy with Hilton. Between the two programs, you’ll have pet-friendly options almost anywhere.

My recommendation:

  • Get the IHG Premier card for Kimpton stays ($99/year, free night certificate pays for itself)
  • Get the Hilton Surpass or Aspire for everywhere else
  • Never pay pet fees again
