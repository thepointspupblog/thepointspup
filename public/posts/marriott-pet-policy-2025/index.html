How to Find Pet-Friendly Marriott Hotels

Marriott’s website makes it easy to filter for pet-friendly properties:

Tips for Staying at Marriott with Your Dog

Before you book:

  • Call the specific hotel to confirm pet policy
  • Ask about weight limits, breed restrictions, and fees
  • Request a ground-floor room near an exit
  • Ask if there’s a designated pet relief area

At check-in:

  • Bring your dog’s vaccination records (rarely asked, but good to have)
  • Pay the pet fee (usually charged to your card at check-in)
  • Ask about pet relief areas and nearby parks
  • Get the front desk number in case you need anything

During your stay:

  • Never leave your dog unattended in the room for long periods
  • Keep your dog leashed in all public areas
  • Use the “Do Not Disturb” sign if your dog is in the room
  • Clean up after your dog promptly
