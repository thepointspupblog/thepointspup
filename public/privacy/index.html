Privacy Policy

2 minute read

Last updated: December 2024

The Short Version

I collect minimal data, I don’t sell your information, and I use cookies only for basic site functionality and analytics.

Information I Collect

If you email me: I’ll have your email address and whatever you share in your message. I use this only to respond to you.

Automatically: Like most websites, this site collects basic analytics data — things like what pages you visit, how long you stay, and what device you’re using. This helps me understand what content is useful.

Cookies

This site uses cookies for:

  • Analytics — to see how people use the site (I use privacy-friendly analytics)
  • Affiliate links — when you click a credit card or product link, the affiliate program may set a cookie to track the referral

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

Affiliate Links & Third Parties

Some links on this site are affiliate links. When you click them, you may be redirected to a third-party site (like a credit card issuer or Amazon) that has its own privacy policy. I’m not responsible for their practices.

Affiliate partners I work with may include:

  • Credit card affiliate networks
  • Amazon Associates
  • Hotel booking partners

Your Data

I don’t sell your personal information. Ever.

If you’ve emailed me and want your information deleted, just ask.

Changes

I may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
