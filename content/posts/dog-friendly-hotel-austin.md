## Dog Parks

Austin has incredible off-leash options â€” including places where dogs can swim.

### Zilker Park Off-Leash Area

The most famous dog park in Austin, located within Zilker Park near Barton Springs.

- **Address:** 2100 Barton Springs Rd, Austin, TX 78704
- **Features:** Large open fields, creek access for swimming, shaded areas
- **Hours:** 5am-10pm
- **Pro tip:** "Barking Springs" is the unofficial name for the area below Barton Springs Pool where dogs can swim in the creek

### Red Bud Isle

A 13-acre off-leash island park on Lady Bird Lake. One of the best dog parks in the country.

- **Address:** 3401 Red Bud Trail, Austin, TX 78746
- **Features:** Trails, swimming in Lady Bird Lake, completely off-leash
- **Hours:** 5am-10pm
- **Note:** The entire island is off-leash. Dogs can swim anywhere along the shoreline.

### Auditorium Shores Off-Leash Area

Downtown location right on Lady Bird Lake with swimming access.

- **Address:** 800 W Riverside Dr, Austin, TX 78704
- **Features:** Lake swimming, skyline views, close to downtown
- **Distance from Kimpton:** ~10 min walk

### Norwood Estate Dog Park

Smaller but well-maintained park with separate areas for large and small dogs.

- **Address:** 1009 Edgecliff Terrace, Austin, TX 78704
- **Features:** Fenced, shaded, water fountains

### Bull Creek District Park

If your dog loves water, this is the spot. Multiple creek access points for swimming and wading.

- **Address:** 6701 Lakewood Dr, Austin, TX 78731
- **Note:** On-leash in most areas, but dogs can access the creek

Massive outdoor space with food, beer, and dogs everywhere.

## What Else to Do

### Barton Springs Pool

Dogs aren't allowed in the pool itself, but "Barking Springs" (the area just downstream) is an unofficial off-leash swimming spot that's been a local tradition for decades.

### Lady Bird Lake Hike & Bike Trail

10+ miles of trails around the lake. Dogs must be on-leash, but it's a beautiful walk with downtown skyline views. Multiple spots to access the water.

### Mount Bonnell

Short but steep hike with incredible views. Dogs allowed on-leash.

### South Congress (SoCo)

Walk the strip, pop into dog-friendly shops, grab tacos. Very dog-friendly neighborhood.

### Domain NORTHSIDE

Outdoor shopping district where dogs are welcome. Multiple dog-friendly restaurants and shops.
